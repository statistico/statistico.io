The building blocks of Statistico

What is Statistico

Statistico is the statistics-led emotion-free automated trading experience. We actively build and maintain a collection of applications that use football statistics and machine learning models to determine the probabilities of sporting events occurring such as goals scored, number of corners, total fouls and/or cards in a football match.

Learn From Mistakes

Many failed traders fail due to human initiated errors. Human emotions and impulses can lead to erratic decision-making, trades placed on “gut feeling”, normally leading to larger than normal stakes placed. Combine this with no real long term strategy leads to a lack of consistency and many a trader’s bankroll diminishes when the dreaded and inevitable chase hits.

Our Philosophy

Statistico is an ecosystem that eliminates all human impulse providing consistency from machine led selections through to automated strategy processes removing emotion and impulse from every step of the pipeline. Statistico is an experience that leans heavily on letting the machine, not the human, do the work.

Stats Driven Consistency

Monitoring key historical statistics and using machine learning algorithms that continually adapt and improve alongside defined trading strategies, Statistico provides a consistent long term and automated trading solution.
