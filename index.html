About Us

Historical football data is used everyday by a variety of consumers. Traders and recreational punters alike use stats to make informed decisions on the winner, the potential number of cards or even the timing of the first goal in an upcoming match. Fantasy football enthusiasts use recent form data to decide on selecting a certain high performing player as their captain or to transfer a player who is not making the grade.

Whatever the use case, football statistics are fundamental in helping consumers find an edge. Ploughing through excel spreadsheets and an endless list of numbers can prove to be a thankless and often time consuming task.

Statistico provides the tools to alleviate this by providing user friendly graphical representations of recent and historical data that is fully filterable. Raw data is converted into visually aesthetic representations allowing users to visually understand and interpret specific statistics. Doing so allows users to make more informed decisions with a few clicks of a button.
